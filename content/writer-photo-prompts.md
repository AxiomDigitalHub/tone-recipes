# Writer Profile Photo Prompts

Style note: All prompts target a consistent editorial portrait style — warm lighting, shallow depth of field, natural environment. These should feel like author headshots for a music magazine, not LinkedIn photos.

---

## 1. Rick Dalton — The Analog Patriarch

Portrait photo of a rugged white man in his early 60s with weathered features and silver-gray hair, slightly long and pushed back. Short stubble beard, deep laugh lines. Wearing a faded black band t-shirt under an open flannel shirt. Standing in a dimly lit rehearsal space with a wall of tube amplifiers slightly blurred behind him. Arms crossed, confident but not arrogant. Warm amber lighting from the side. He looks like he's been gigging since the Carter administration and has no plans to stop. Editorial portrait, shallow depth of field, 85mm lens feel.

## 2. Jess Kowalski — The Punk Engineer

Portrait photo of a young woman in her mid-20s with choppy dyed hair (dark roots with bleached or teal-tipped ends), multiple ear piercings, and an easygoing smirk. ## Style Consistency Notes

For a cohesive look across all 10:
- Same aspect ratio (3:4 or 4:5 portrait orientation)
- Same shallow depth of field / bokeh style
- All shot from roughly chest-up
- Warm-leaning color grade across the set (even Viktor's cooler shot should feel like it belongs)
- No one looking directly at camera with a corporate smile — these should feel candid-editorial, like a photographer caught them mid-thought
- No visible brand logos on clothing or gear
